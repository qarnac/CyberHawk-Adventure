CyberHawk Adventure
This adventure provides a close-up exploration of the geological features, habitats and resources located in the Anza Borrego State Park through the Google Earth browser plugin. Use keyboard controls on your computer to fly a hawk through the park region and investigate specific defined locations that contain photos, directions, clues and/or questions. Points are earned during the tour that may be printed as evidence of your visit. Have fun on your tour!
